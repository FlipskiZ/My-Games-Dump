CONTROLS:

MAIN GAME:

Arrow keys - Movement

WASD - Weapon

E - Melee (if you have a melee weapon)

B - Bandage (if you have bandages)

V - Stamina Pill (if you have stamina pills)

R - Reload (happens automatically when the clip is empty)

P - Pause (When paused, you can then press R to restart the game)

T - Skilltree

SPACE - Shop (has to be on the dollar symbol)



SHOP:

Arrow keys - selection

Space - Buy item



SKILLTREE

ARROW KEYS - selection

SPACE - Buy skill
